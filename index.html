About Me

Hi there! I'm Bryan, a passionate archivist with a strong background in technology. I bridge the gap between traditional archival practices and modern digital solutions. When I'm not preserving history, you'll find me tinkering with Linux, coding in C++ and Rust, or finding something else tech related to explore.

About This Site

When I was studying for my first degree I made extensive use of tutorial websites that no longer exist to understand it all. Now that I no longer work in software development am trying to keep my skills sharp I felt it important to try to pay the efforts of all those programmers that helped me forward. This page first started as a portfolio piece and a home for my writing but it will also house any tutorials I come up with using whatever language I happen to be interested in at the time.
